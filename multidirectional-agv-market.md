<p><p>The Multidirectional Automated Guided Vehicle (AGV) market is primarily divided into two types: Optical Guiding and Magnetic Guiding. Optical Guiding AGVs utilize laser or vision systems for navigation, allowing them to follow predefined paths or recognize landmarks, providing greater flexibility and adaptability in dynamic environments. In contrast, Magnetic Guiding AGVs rely on magnetic strips or markers embedded in the floor for navigation, offering a reliable and cost-effective solution for structured environments. Both types enhance efficiency in material handling and logistics.</p></p>
