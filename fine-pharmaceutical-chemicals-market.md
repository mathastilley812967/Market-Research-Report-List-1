<p><p>The Fine Pharmaceutical Chemicals market is categorized into two main types: small molecules and big molecules. In contrast, big molecules, or biologics, are larger, complex proteins or nucleic acids produced through biological processes, such as fermentation or cell culture. These are often used for targeted therapies and have transformed treatments for various diseases.</p></p>
