<p><ul><li>Delay-line type</li><li>Resonators Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surface Acoustic Wave (SAW) biosensors can be categorized into two main types: delay-line type and resonator type. Delay-line SAW biosensors utilize the time delay of acoustic waves traveling along a substrate, detecting changes in mass or refractive index due to biomolecular interactions. In contrast, resonator-type SAW biosensors focus on variations in resonance frequency resulting from these interactions. Both types offer high sensitivity and real-time detection, making them valuable in medical diagnostics and environmental monitoring applications.</p></p>
