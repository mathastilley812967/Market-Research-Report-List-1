<p><p>The oven bag market comprises primarily two types: polyester and nylon. Polyester oven bags are known for their durability and resistance to high temperatures, making them suitable for various cooking methods. They are often used for roasting and steaming, ensuring even heat distribution. Nylon oven bags, while slightly less heat-resistant, offer flexibility and transparency, allowing for easy monitoring of cooking progress. Both materials cater to consumer preferences for convenient, mess-free cooking solutions, contributing to their growing popularity in kitchens.</p></p>
