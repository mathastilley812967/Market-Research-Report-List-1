<p><strong>The Commercial Coastal Radar Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>X-Band Radar</li><li>S-Band Radar</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Commercial Coastal Radar market encompasses various radar types, with X-Band and S-Band being the primary categories. X-Band Radar operates at higher frequencies, providing high-resolution imaging suitable for detecting smaller objects and detailed coastal monitoring, making it ideal for applications like port security and fishing activities. In contrast, S-Band Radar functions at lower frequencies, offering broader coverage and better performance in adverse weather conditions, which makes it valuable for maritime traffic control and weather surveillance. Together, they enhance coastal safety and operational efficiency.</p></p>
