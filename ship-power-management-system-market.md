<p><ul><li>Power Generation Systems</li><li>Power Distribution System</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Ship Power Management System market consists of two key types: Power Generation Systems and Power Distribution Systems. Power Generation Systems encompass technologies that produce electrical energy aboard ships, including generators, turbines, and renewable energy sources. In contrast, Power Distribution Systems focus on the efficient transmission of that generated power throughout the vessel, ensuring that all electrical components and systems receive the necessary energy. Together, these systems enhance operational efficiency, reliability, and safety in marine environments.</p></p>
