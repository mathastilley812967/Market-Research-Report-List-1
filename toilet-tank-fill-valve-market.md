<p><strong>The Toilet Tank Fill Valve Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Float Ball</li><li>Float Cup</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The toilet tank fill valve market primarily features two types: float ball and float cup valves. Float ball valves consist of a large ball that rises and falls with the water level, controlling the water flow to the tank. In contrast, float cup valves use a cylindrical float that moves up and down along a guide, providing a more compact design and efficient operation. Both types serve the essential function of refilling the toilet tank after a flush, ensuring reliable performance in household plumbing.</p></p>
