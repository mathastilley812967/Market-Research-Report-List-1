<p><p>The Ethernet cable market includes various categories, each designed for specific networking needs. Cat 5 cables support speeds up to 100 Mbps and are suited for basic applications. Cat 5e offers enhanced performance with reduced crosstalk, enabling speeds up to 1 Gbps. Cat 6 cables improve on this, supporting 10 Gbps over shorter distances, ideal for modern networks. Cat 7 cables further enhance performance, offering shielding for reduced interference and supporting higher speeds, making them suitable for data centers and high-demand environments.</p></p>
