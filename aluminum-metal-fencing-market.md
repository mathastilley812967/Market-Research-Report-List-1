<p><p>The aluminum metal fencing market is primarily categorized into two types: mesh and strip fencing. Mesh fencing consists of interconnected aluminum wires or rods that create a sturdy yet open structure, ideal for security and visibility. Strip fencing, on the other hand, utilizes solid aluminum panels or slats, providing more privacy and a sleek appearance. Both types are valued for their durability, lightweight nature, resistance to corrosion, and low maintenance, making them popular choices in residential and commercial applications.</p></p>
