<p><p>The AC-DC power conversion market consists of two main types: external AC-DC power supplies and embedded AC-DC power supplies. External AC-DC power supplies are standalone adapters or chargers that convert alternating current (AC) from the wall outlet to direct current (DC) for electronic devices. In contrast, embedded AC-DC power supplies are integrated within products, providing efficient power conversion directly inside devices such as computers and appliances. Both types cater to varying applications, with an emphasis on efficiency, size, and performance.</p></p>
