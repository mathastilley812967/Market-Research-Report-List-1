<p><p>The powdered bakery enzyme market comprises three main types: bakery protease enzymes, bakery carbohydrase enzymes, and bakery lipase enzymes. Bakery protease enzymes enhance dough strength and improve texture by breaking down proteins, resulting in softer baked goods. Bakery carbohydrase enzymes facilitate the breakdown of carbohydrates, aiding fermentation and enhancing dough volume. Bakery lipase enzymes improve the flavor, texture, and shelf life of baked products by breaking down fats. Together, these enzymes optimize baking processes and elevate product quality.</p></p>
