<p><ul><li>Nutritive Sweeteners</li><li>Non-nutritive Sweeteners</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The pharmaceutical sweeteners market is categorized into two primary types: nutritive sweeteners and non-nutritive sweeteners. Nutritive sweeteners, such as glucose and sucrose, provide calories and can enhance the taste of medications while contributing to energy intake. Non-nutritive sweeteners, including aspartame and stevia, offer sweetness without significant calories, making them suitable for sugar-restricted diets. Both categories play crucial roles in improving patient adherence to medication by enhancing flavor and masking unpleasant tastes inherent in certain pharmaceutical formulations.</p></p>
