<p><p>The electrical power transformer market is categorized into three key types: low voltage, medium voltage, and high voltage transformers. Low voltage transformers typically handle voltages up to 1 kV, primarily used in residential and commercial applications. Medium voltage transformers operate between 1 kV and 36 kV, serving industrial facilities and utility distribution systems. High voltage transformers manage voltages above 36 kV, essential for long-distance power transmission and high-capacity substations, facilitating efficient energy distribution across extensive grids.</p></p>
