<p><strong>The Laser Theodolite Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Transit Theodolite</li><li>Non-transit Theodolite</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Laser Theodolite market is primarily segmented into two types: transit and non-transit theodolites. Transit theodolites allow for both horizontal and vertical angle measurements with the capability of viewing the scale from either side, offering versatility in surveying applications. In contrast, non-transit theodolites are typically simpler, with a fixed telescope position, making them easier to use for basic measurements. Both types utilize laser technology for enhanced precision and efficiency in construction, engineering, and geodetic work.</p></p>
