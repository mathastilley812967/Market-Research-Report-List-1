<p><p>The Truck and Bus Wheel Speed Sensor market comprises two primary types: Hall effect sensors and magnetic electric sensors. Hall effect sensors use magnetic fields to detect the rotational speed of wheels, providing precise data for anti-lock braking systems and traction control. Magnetic electric sensors, on the other hand, generate voltage signals as they detect movement, converting mechanical energy into electrical signals. Both types are essential for enhancing vehicle safety and performance by enabling accurate speed monitoring and control systems.</p></p>
