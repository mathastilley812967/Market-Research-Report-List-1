<p><p>Handheld Optical Time Domain Reflectometers (OTDRs) are essential tools for testing and troubleshooting fiber optic networks. The market is segmented into low-frequency and high-frequency reflectometers. Low-frequency reflectometers typically operate at wavelengths below 1550 nm and are suited for shorter-distance applications, offering a balance of cost and performance. In contrast, high-frequency reflectometers operate at higher wavelengths, enabling more precise measurements over longer distances, making them ideal for complex networks. Both types cater to different user needs in the telecommunications industry.</p></p>
