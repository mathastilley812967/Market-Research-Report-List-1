<p><p>The depth filtration media market comprises several key types, including activated carbon, diatomaceous earth, cellulose, and perlite. Activated carbon is used for adsorption of impurities and odor removal. Diatomaceous earth serves as a natural filter aid, effective in capturing fine particles. Cellulose filters are biodegradable and offer high flow rates, commonly used in food and beverage industries. Perlite, a volcanic glass, provides excellent thermal insulation and is used in various filtration applications due to its low density and porosity.</p></p>
