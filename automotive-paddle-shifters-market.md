<p><ul><li>Fixed Paddle Shifters</li><li>Follow-up Paddle Shifters</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive paddle shifters market comprises two main types: fixed paddle shifters and follow-up paddle shifters. Fixed paddle shifters remain stationary and are mounted on the steering column, allowing drivers to shift gears while keeping their hands on the wheel. In contrast, follow-up paddle shifters are attached to the steering wheel and rotate with it, providing a more ergonomic experience. Both types enhance driver engagement and control, particularly in performance-oriented vehicles, contributing to the growing popularity of semi-automatic transmissions.</p></p>
