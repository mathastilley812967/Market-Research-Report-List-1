<p><p>The polyester fibers market comprises two main types: polyester staple fiber (PSF) and polyester filament yarn (PFY). PSF is a short, staple-length fiber typically used in textiles, upholstery, and non-woven applications, valued for its durability and comfort. PFY, on the other hand, consists of continuous strands used primarily in high-performance textiles and industrial applications, offering strength and elasticity. Both types are integral to the apparel, automotive, and home furnishing industries, driven by demand for lightweight, resistant materials.</p></p>
