<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Aluminum Alloy Extrusion Profiles Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Forward Extrusion</li><li>Backward Extrusion</li><li>Composite Extrusion</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Aluminum alloy extrusion profiles are shaped by forcing aluminum through a die. **Forward Extrusion** involves pushing the aluminum billet through the die in the same direction as the ram, creating profiles with consistent cross-sections. **Backward Extrusion** pushes the billet against a stationary die, forming hollow or complex shapes. **Composite Extrusion** combines multiple materials or processes, enhancing properties like strength or corrosion resistance. Each method caters to specific applications, offering versatility in industries like construction, automotive, and aerospace.</p></p>
