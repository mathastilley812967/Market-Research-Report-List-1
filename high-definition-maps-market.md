<p><p>High-definition maps (HD maps) can be categorized into two main market types: centralized and crowdsourced. Centralized mapping involves professional organizations that create and maintain detailed, high-accuracy maps, often leveraging advanced technologies and infrastructure. In contrast, crowdsourcing relies on data contributions from individuals or a community, enhancing map accuracy and coverage over time. While centralized maps prioritize precision and consistency, crowdsourced maps promote real-time updates and local insights, making both essential for applications like autonomous driving and navigation.</p></p>
