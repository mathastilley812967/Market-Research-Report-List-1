<p><strong>The Metallic Paint Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Solvent Based</li><li>Water Based</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The metallic paint market is segmented into solvent-based and water-based types. Solvent-based metallic paints use organic solvents as carriers, offering durability, high gloss, and resistance to harsh conditions, making them ideal for automotive and industrial applications. Water-based metallic paints, on the other hand, use water as a carrier, providing eco-friendliness, low VOC emissions, and easier cleanup, suitable for residential and commercial use. Both types deliver a metallic finish but differ in environmental impact, application ease, and performance characteristics.</p></p>
