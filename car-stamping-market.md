<p><strong>The Car Stamping Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Hot Stamping</li><li>Cold Stamping</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The car stamping market includes **hot stamping** and **cold stamping**, both used to shape metal parts for vehicles. **Hot stamping** involves heating steel to high temperatures, making it easier to form complex, high-strength components like safety-critical parts. It enhances durability and reduces weight. **Cold stamping** shapes metal at room temperature, ideal for simpler, cost-effective parts like body panels. Both methods are essential in automotive manufacturing, with hot stamping focusing on strength and safety, while cold stamping prioritizes efficiency and affordability.</p></p>
