<p><p>Heterojunction batteries (HIT) feature a combination of different semiconductor materials to enhance efficiency and performance. The market mainly consists of three types: </p><p>1. **SHJ (Silicon Heterojunction)**: Combines crystalline silicon with an amorphous silicon layer, improving efficiency and reducing energy loss. </p><p>2. **HDT (Heterojunction with Double Transparent Conductive Oxide)**: Utilizes a double layer of transparent conductive oxides to further enhance light absorption and electrical conductivity. </p><p>3. **Others**: Encompasses various innovative designs and hybrid solutions aimed at optimizing energy conversion and storage, enhancing competitiveness in renewable energy markets.</p></p>
