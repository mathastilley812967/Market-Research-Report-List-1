<p><strong>The Fuel Injectors Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Throttle Body Injection</li><li>Multiport Injection</li><li>Direct Injection</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Fuel Injectors Market includes three main types: Throttle Body Injection (TBI), Multiport Injection (MPI), and Direct Injection (DI). TBI injects fuel into the throttle body, mixing it with air before entering the engine, offering simplicity and cost-effectiveness. MPI delivers fuel to individual cylinders via multiple injectors, improving efficiency and performance. DI injects fuel directly into the combustion chamber, enhancing power, fuel economy, and emissions control. Each type caters to varying automotive needs, balancing performance, cost, and environmental compliance.</p></p>
