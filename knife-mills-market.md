<p><p>The knife mills market is segmented into two main types: pulley knife mills and without pulley knife mills. </p><p>Pulley knife mills utilize a pulley system to drive the blades, enabling efficient grinding and shredding of materials. This design enhances performance and allows for adjustable speed settings. </p><p>In contrast, without pulley knife mills operate with a direct drive mechanism, offering a more compact and straightforward design. They are typically easier to maintain and can be more cost-effective for specific applications, appealing to various industries.</p></p>
