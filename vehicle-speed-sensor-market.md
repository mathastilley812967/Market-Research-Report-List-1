<p><ul><li>Hall Type</li><li>Magnetic Electric Type</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Vehicle Speed Sensor (VSS) market consists primarily of two types: Hall Effect and Magnetic Electric sensors. Hall Effect sensors utilize a magnetic field to produce a voltage signal proportional to the speed of the vehicle, offering high precision and reliability. Meanwhile, Magnetic Electric sensors rely on the generation of an electric current as a magnet passes by, making them cost-effective for various applications. Both types serve critical roles in modern automotive systems for speed monitoring and electronic control.</p></p>
