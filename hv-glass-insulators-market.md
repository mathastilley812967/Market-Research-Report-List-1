<p><ul><li>Suspension Glass Insulators</li><li>Pin Glass Insulators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The HV Glass Insulators market features primarily two types: Suspension Glass Insulators and Pin Glass Insulators. Suspension Glass Insulators are designed to support overhead power lines at higher voltages, offering durability and minimizing electrical losses. They are typically used in transmission lines. Conversely, Pin Glass Insulators are mounted vertically on poles, providing insulation for lower voltage distribution lines. Both types are crucial for enhancing electrical system reliability and ensuring safety in power transmission and distribution networks.</p></p>
