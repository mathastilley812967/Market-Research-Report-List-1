<p><ul><li>Modified Sine Wave Type</li><li>Pure Sine Wave</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The automotive power inverters market is primarily segmented into modified sine wave and pure sine wave types. Modified sine wave inverters produce a waveform that approximates a sine wave but includes more harmonic distortion, making them suitable for simpler electronic devices and less expensive. In contrast, pure sine wave inverters generate a smooth, continuous waveform that closely mimics utility power, making them ideal for sensitive electronics and high-performance applications, ensuring compatibility and efficient operation.</p></p>
