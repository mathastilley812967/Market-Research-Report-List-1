<p><strong>The Furniture Panels Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Particleboard (PB)</li><li>Medium density fibreboard (MDF)</li><li>High density fibreboard (HDF)</li><li>Oriented strand board (OSB)</li><li>Plywood</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The furniture panels market consists of several types: </p><p>1. **Particleboard (PB)**: Made from wood chips and resin, it’s economical and commonly used for low-cost furniture. </p><p>2. **Medium Density Fibreboard (MDF)**: Composed of wood fibers, MDF offers a smooth finish, making it ideal for veneers and detailed designs. </p><p>3. **High Density Fibreboard (HDF)**: Denser than MDF, HDF provides durability, often used for flooring and high-end furniture. </p><p>4. **Oriented Strand Board (OSB)**: Engineered with strands of wood for strength, commonly used in construction. </p><p>5. **Plywood**: Made from layers of veneer, it offers exceptional strength and stability, used in both furniture and structural applications.</p></p>
