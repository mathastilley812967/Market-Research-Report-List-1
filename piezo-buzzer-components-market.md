<p><ul><li>Active Piezo Buzzer</li><li>Passive Piezo Buzzer</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The piezo buzzer components market is primarily divided into two types: active and passive piezo buzzers. Active piezo buzzers contain built-in oscillators, enabling them to produce sound with a direct power source, making them easier to use and often smaller. In contrast, passive piezo buzzers require an external audio frequency signal to generate sound, providing more versatility in tone modulation but requiring additional circuitry. Both types find applications in alarms, home appliances, and various electronic devices.</p></p>
