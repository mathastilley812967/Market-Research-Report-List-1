In contrast, the cracking method breaks down larger hydrocarbon molecules into smaller ones, generating decylene as a byproduct. Both methods serve distinct industrial needs, influencing the availability, pricing, and quality of decylene in various markets, including cosmetics and plastics.</p></p>
