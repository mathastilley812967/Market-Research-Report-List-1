Dynamic monitoring employs wearable devices to track physiological parameters like heart rate and movement, offering a comprehensive view of sleep quality. Video monitoring uses cameras to capture visual data on sleeping behavior, enhancing understanding of disturbances or movements during the night, and providing holistic sleep assessments.</p></p>
