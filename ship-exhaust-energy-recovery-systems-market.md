<p><strong>The Ship Exhaust Energy Recovery Systems Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Single-stage Radial</li><li>Single-stage Axial</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Ship Exhaust Energy Recovery Systems (EERS) focus on capturing waste heat from engine exhaust to enhance fuel efficiency. The Single-stage Radial system uses a centrifugal compressor to boost exhaust pressure, effectively converting heat into usable energy. In contrast, the Single-stage Axial system employs an axial compressor, allowing for continuous airflow, which is more efficient at higher power levels and is better suited for larger vessels. Both types aim to reduce emissions and optimize energy usage aboard ships.</p></p>
