Capacitive level sensors detect changes in capacitance due to material presence. Ultrasonic sensors use sound waves to gauge level by measuring time delay. Radar level sensors utilize electromagnetic waves for precise measurements, unaffected by temperature or pressure. Other types may include innovative technologies like laser or optical sensors. Each type offers distinct advantages suited for specific industrial requirements and environments.</p></p>
