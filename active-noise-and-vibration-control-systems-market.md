ANC Systems utilize microphones and speakers to detect and counteract unwanted noise, enhancing comfort in environments like automotive and aerospace. Conversely, AVC Systems employ sensors and actuators to minimize vibrations, improving equipment longevity and performance in industries such as manufacturing and aerospace. Both systems enhance user experience by creating quieter and more stable environments.</p></p>
