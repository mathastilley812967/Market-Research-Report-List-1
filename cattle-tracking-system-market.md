<p><p>Cattle tracking systems utilize various technologies to monitor livestock movements and health. Radio-based systems rely on low-frequency signals for short-range tracking, ideal for confined spaces like farms. Each type offers unique advantages for managing livestock and enhancing operational efficiency.</p></p>
