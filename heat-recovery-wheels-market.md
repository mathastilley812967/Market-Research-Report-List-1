<p><ul><li>Condensation Rotors</li><li>Hygroscopic Rotors</li><li>Sorption Rotors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The heat recovery wheels market includes three main types: condensation rotors, hygroscopic rotors, and sorption rotors. Condensation rotors utilize temperature differences to transfer heat from exhaust air, while hygroscopic rotors absorb moisture and heat to enhance energy recovery in humid environments. Sorption rotors employ chemical processes to capture and release heat, offering efficient thermal management. Each type serves distinct applications in HVAC systems, improving energy efficiency and reducing operational costs in both residential and commercial settings.</p></p>
