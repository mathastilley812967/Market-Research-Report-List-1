<p><p>Manufacturing Execution Systems (MES) are crucial for managing and monitoring manufacturing processes in real-time. The market can be segmented into three types: On-Premises MES solutions are installed locally on a company’s servers, providing high control and security but requiring significant IT resources. On-Demand MES, also known as cloud-based, offers flexibility and scalability, allowing users to access the system via the internet with lower upfront costs. Hybrid MES combines both approaches, enabling businesses to leverage on-premises reliability with cloud-based accessibility.</p></p>
