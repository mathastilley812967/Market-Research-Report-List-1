<p><p>The Miniature Thermoelectric Modules (TEM) market is segmented into Single Stage and Multistage types. Single Stage Miniature Thermoelectric Modules utilize a single thermoelectric junction, providing efficient cooling or heating for compact applications. They are ideal for portable devices. In contrast, Multistage Miniature Thermoelectric Modules consist of multiple thermoelectric junctions stacked together, enabling greater temperature differentials and improved performance for demanding applications. These modules find use in advanced cooling solutions, electronics, and precision temperature control systems.</p></p>
