<p><p>The plaque disclosing market comprises three main types: plaque disclosing tablets, solutions, and swabs. Tablets are chewable and release dyes that highlight dental plaque, making it visible after chewing. Solutions are liquid formulations applied directly to teeth, revealing plaque presence through staining. Swabs, typically pre-coated with dye, are used to manually apply the stain, providing a targeted approach. These products are utilized in dental hygiene practices to enhance oral health awareness and promote effective brushing techniques.</p></p>
