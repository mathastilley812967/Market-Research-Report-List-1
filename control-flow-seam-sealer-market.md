<p><p>The Control Flow Seam Sealer market is categorized into two main types: single-component and two-component sealers. Single-component sealers are ready-to-use products that offer convenience and consistent performance, typically requiring no mixing. In contrast, two-component sealers consist of a base and a hardener that must be mixed before application, allowing for enhanced flexibility and adhesion properties. Each type caters to different application needs, with single-component sealers focusing on ease and speed, while two-component options provide superior durability and strength.</p></p>
