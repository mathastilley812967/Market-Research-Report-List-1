<p><strong>The Investment Trust Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Bond Trust</li><li>Equity Trust</li><li>Income-based Trust</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Investment trusts are categorized into three main market types. Bond trusts primarily invest in fixed-income securities, aiming for stable returns through interest payments. Equity trusts focus on stocks, seeking capital appreciation by investing in various companies, providing potential for higher growth. Income-based trusts, on the other hand, emphasize generating regular income through dividends or interest, often blending both bond and equity investments to achieve a balance between stability and growth. Each type addresses different investor goals and risk tolerances.</p></p>
