<p><ul><li>Air-Cooled</li><li>Water-Cooled</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The modular chillers market primarily consists of two types: air-cooled and water-cooled systems. Air-cooled chillers use ambient air to dissipate heat, making them easier to install and requiring less maintenance, suitable for smaller applications. In contrast, water-cooled chillers rely on water sources for heat exchange, offering higher efficiency and capacity for larger installations, but necessitating a cooling tower or dedicated water supply. Both types are essential for efficient cooling in various commercial and industrial settings.</p></p>
