<p><p>The 3-piece ball valves market is segmented into manual, pneumatic, and electric types, each serving distinct applications. Manual ball valves are operated by hand, offering a straightforward solution for applications requiring infrequent adjustments. Pneumatic ball valves utilize compressed air for actuation, ideal for automated systems where rapid response and remote control are essential. Electric ball valves, powered by electric actuators, provide precise control and automation capabilities, suitable for complex processes needing consistent monitoring and adjustment. Each type meets varying operational demands and efficiency requirements.</p></p>
