<p><p>The microwave diodes market includes several key types, each serving distinct applications. Schottky diodes are favored for high-frequency applications due to their fast switching capabilities. Gunn diodes are used in microwave oscillators and amplifiers due to their unique negative resistance properties. Tunnel diodes excel in high-speed switching and low-voltage applications. Zener diodes provide voltage regulation in microwave circuits. PIN diodes are utilized in radio frequency switching and signal modulation. Together, these diodes play crucial roles in telecommunications, radar, and other microwave technologies.</p></p>
