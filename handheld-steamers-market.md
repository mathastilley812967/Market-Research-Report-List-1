<p><p>The handheld steamers market can be categorized into two main types: pointed handheld steamers and round head handheld steamers. Pointed handheld steamers feature a tapered nozzle, allowing for precise steaming in tight spaces and intricate garment details. This design is ideal for delicate fabrics and hard-to-reach areas. In contrast, round head handheld steamers provide a broader steam coverage, making them suitable for larger surfaces and quicker steaming of garments. Both types cater to diverse consumer needs in clothing care and home maintenance.</p></p>
