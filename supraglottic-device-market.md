<p><p>The supraglottic device market includes several types: </p><p>1. **Laryngeal Mask Airways (LMAs)** - these provide a secure airway by fitting over the laryngeal inlet, ideal for anesthesia and emergencies.</p><p>2. **Oropharyngeal Airways (OPAs)** - designed to maintain airway patency by preventing tongue obstruction, commonly used in unconscious patients.</p><p>3. **Nasopharyngeal Airways (NPAs)** - inserted through the nose to the pharynx, effective in conscious patients requiring airway support.</p><p>4. **Others** - includes diverse supraglottic devices designed for specialized or less common applications.</p></p>
