<p><ul><li>EPTFE Vaso Transplantation</li><li>Polyester Vaso Transplantation</li><li>PTFE Vaso Transplantation</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Vaso transplantation involves surgical procedures to replace or repair damaged blood vessels using synthetic grafts. The market includes EPTFE (expanded polytetrafluoroethylene), polyester, and PTFE (polytetrafluoroethylene) grafts. EPTFE grafts are known for their biocompatibility and durability, often used in cardiovascular surgeries. Polyester grafts, typically woven or knitted, are flexible and widely used for aortic repairs. PTFE grafts, similar to EPTFE, offer excellent blood flow compatibility and are used in peripheral vascular surgeries. Each type caters to specific clinical needs, ensuring effective vascular repair and replacement.</p></p>
