<p><strong>Market Segmentation</strong></p>
<p><strong>The Arsenic Removal Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Precipitative Process</li><li>Adsorptive Process</li><li>Ion Exchange Process</li><li>Membrane Process</li><li>Others</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The arsenic removal market encompasses several key types of processes: </p><p>1. **Precipitative Process**: This technique involves chemical reactions that convert dissolved arsenic into solid particles, which are then removed from water. </p><p>2. **Adsorptive Process**: Utilizes materials like activated carbon or iron-based adsorbents to capture arsenic ions from water.</p><p>3. **Ion Exchange Process**: Involves exchanging arsenic ions with harmless ions using resins. </p><p>4. **Membrane Process**: Employs filtration membranes to separate arsenic from water based on size and charge.</p><p>5. **Others**: Includes emerging technologies like electrocoagulation and advanced oxidation processes for effective arsenic removal.</p></p>
