<p><strong>The X Ray Ore Sorter Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li> XRT Ore Sorter</li><li>XRF Ore Sorter </li></ul></p>
<p><p>The X-ray Ore Sorter market is primarily divided into two types: XRT (X-ray Transmission) and XRF (X-ray Fluorescence) ore sorters. XRT ore sorters utilize transmitted X-rays to differentiate materials based on density and atomic number, effectively separating valuable minerals from waste. Conversely, XRF ore sorters analyze the fluorescence emitted by materials when exposed to X-rays, enabling precise identification and classification of elemental composition. Both technologies enhance mining efficiency and resource recovery, catering to the growing demand for sustainable mineral processing.</p></p>
