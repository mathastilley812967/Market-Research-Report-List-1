<p><p>The neurodiagnostic and monitoring devices market encompasses various types of equipment utilized for assessing brain and nerve functions. Electroencephalogram (EEG) devices measure electrical activity in the brain, while electromyography (EMG) assesses muscle response by measuring electrical signals. Transcranial Doppler evaluates blood flow in the brain's major arteries. Intracranial pressure monitors track pressure within the skull, and cerebral oximeters measure oxygen levels in brain tissue. Together, these devices are essential for diagnosing neurological conditions and monitoring patient health.</p></p>
