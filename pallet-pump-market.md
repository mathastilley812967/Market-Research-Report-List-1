<p><p>The pallet pump market is primarily divided into two types: manual pallet trucks and electric pallet trucks. Manual pallet trucks operate using human effort, allowing for easy maneuvering of pallets in warehouses and retail spaces. They are cost-effective and require minimal maintenance. In contrast, electric pallet trucks utilize battery power for movement and lifting, enhancing efficiency and reducing operator fatigue, particularly in high-traffic environments. Both types cater to diverse operational needs in material handling and logistics sectors.</p></p>
