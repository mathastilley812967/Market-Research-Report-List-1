<p><strong>The Impregnation Resins Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Type I</li><li>Type II</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Impregnation resins are categorized into two main types: Type I and Type II. Type I impregnation resins typically exhibit lower viscosity, making them suitable for applications where deep penetration into porous materials is essential. They are often used for structural reinforcement. Type II impregnation resins, on the other hand, have higher viscosity, providing better surface coating and enhanced durability. These resins are commonly employed in applications requiring surface protection and resistance to environmental factors, thereby enhancing the longevity of materials.</p></p>
