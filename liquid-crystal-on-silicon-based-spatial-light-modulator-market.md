<p><p>The Liquid Crystal on Silicon (LCoS) based Spatial Light Modulator market is primarily segmented into two types: reflective and transmissive. Reflective Spatial Light Modulators utilize a reflective layer to manipulate light, making them ideal for applications requiring high light efficiency and contrast, such as projectors and displays. In contrast, Transmissive Spatial Light Modulators control light passing through them, suited for applications like imaging and optical processing. Each type offers unique advantages, catering to different technological needs and industry demands.</p></p>
