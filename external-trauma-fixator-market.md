<p><ul><li>Unilateral & Bilateral Fixators</li><li>Circular Fixators</li><li>Hybrid Fixators</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The external trauma fixator market is categorized into several types based on design and application. Unilateral and bilateral fixators are used to stabilize fractures, with unilateral fixators applied on one side of the limb and bilateral supporting both sides. Circular fixators offer a versatile approach, using rings and rods for complex fractures and limb deformities. Hybrid fixators combine features of both unilateral and circular fixators, providing enhanced stability and flexibility for individual patient needs in trauma care.</p></p>
