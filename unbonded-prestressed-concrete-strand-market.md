<p><p>The unbonded prestressed concrete strand market includes three primary types: galvanized steel strand, epoxy coated steel strand, and aluminum clad steel strand. Galvanized steel strands are coated to prevent corrosion, enhancing their durability in harsh environments. Epoxy coated steel strands offer additional protection through a layer of epoxy, reducing the risk of rust. Aluminum clad steel strands combine the strength of steel with the corrosion resistance of aluminum, making them suitable for various applications where durability and performance are essential.</p></p>
