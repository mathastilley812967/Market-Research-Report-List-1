In contrast, the Transcatheter Mitral Valve Replacement (TMVr) market involves the replacement of a diseased mitral valve with a prosthetic valve through catheter-based techniques. Both approaches are minimally invasive, offering patients reduced recovery times and lower risks compared to traditional surgical methods, thereby driving their growing adoption in cardiology.</p></p>
