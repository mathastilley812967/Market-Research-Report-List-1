<p><strong>The String Trimmer Head Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed Head</li><li>Automatic Feed</li><li>Bump Feed</li></ul></p>
<p><p>String trimmer heads are categorized into three main types. Fixed head trimmers use pre-cut line lengths, ideal for minimal maintenance but require manual line replacement. Automatic feed trimmers dispense line as needed, offering convenience without manual intervention, suitable for users seeking efficiency. Bump feed trimmers feature a mechanism that releases additional line when the head is tapped on the ground, allowing for easy length adjustment during operation, catering to users who prefer balance between control and convenience.</p></p>
