<p><ul><li>Centralized IP Surveillance Cameras</li><li>Decentralized IP Surveillance Cameras</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The IP surveillance camera market is divided into two primary types: centralized and decentralized systems. Centralized IP surveillance cameras rely on a single server for processing and storage, offering streamlined management and high-quality video feeds suited for larger setups. In contrast, decentralized IP surveillance systems distribute processing tasks among individual cameras, enhancing flexibility and reducing reliance on a central server. This allows for easier scaling and quicker installation, making it ideal for smaller or dynamic environments where adaptability is crucial.</p></p>
