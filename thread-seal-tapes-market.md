<p><p>Thread seal tapes are categorized into three market types based on density. Standard density tapes are suitable for general plumbing applications, providing adequate sealing for low-pressure systems. High density tapes offer enhanced resistance and durability, making them ideal for medium to high-pressure environments. Full density tapes, being the most robust, are designed for high-pressure and demanding applications, ensuring superior sealing and longevity. Each type serves distinct purposes, catering to varying needs within plumbing and industrial sectors.</p></p>
