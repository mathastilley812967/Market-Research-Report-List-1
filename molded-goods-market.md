<p><p>The molded goods market encompasses various manufacturing techniques, including Liquid Injection Molding (LIM) and Liquid Silicone Rubber (LSR) processes. LIM involves injecting liquid polymer into a mold, creating complex shapes with high precision and durability, making it ideal for automotive and medical applications. LSR, on the other hand, specifically utilizes liquid silicone rubber, offering excellent flexibility, temperature resistance, and biocompatibility, suitable for products like seals, gaskets, and consumer goods. Both techniques cater to diverse industries, emphasizing efficiency and design versatility.</p></p>
