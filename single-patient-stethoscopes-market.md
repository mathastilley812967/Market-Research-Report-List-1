<p><p>The single-patient stethoscopes market is divided into two primary types: manual and automatic stethoscopes. Manual stethoscopes rely on traditional acoustic methods for auscultation, allowing healthcare providers to listen to heart and lung sounds directly. In contrast, automatic stethoscopes utilize advanced technology to amplify sounds, often integrating digital features for enhanced diagnostics. Both types aim to reduce cross-contamination and maintain hygiene by being designated for individual patient use, catering to the growing demand for infection control in healthcare settings.</p></p>
