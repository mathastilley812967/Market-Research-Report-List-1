<p><p>The orthopedic surgical power tools market is categorized into three main types: electric powered, battery operated, and pneumatic powered tools. Electric powered tools are known for their efficiency and consistent performance, making them suitable for complex surgeries. Battery operated tools offer portability and flexibility, ideal for procedures where mobility is essential. Each type serves unique surgical needs.</p></p>
