<p><p>The rheology modifier coating additive market is segmented into three main types: water-based, solvent-based, and powder-based. </p><p>Water-based rheology modifiers are widely used due to their eco-friendliness and ease of application, enhancing paint viscosity and stability. Solvent-based modifiers offer better performance in high-solvent environments, providing excellent flow control and texture.
