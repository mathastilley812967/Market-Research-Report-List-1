<p><strong>The Aircraft Propeller Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Fixed-Pitch Propeller</li><li>Test Club Propeller</li><li>Ground-Adjustable Propeller</li><li>Controllable-Pitch Propeller</li><li>Constant-Speed Propeller</li><li>Feathering Propeller</li><li>Reverse-Pitch Propeller</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The aircraft propeller market includes various types tailored for specific performance needs. Fixed-pitch propellers have a set blade angle, ideal for simple applications. Test club propellers are designed for testing and evaluation. Ground-adjustable propellers allow angle adjustments on the ground. Controllable-pitch propellers enable pilots to adjust blade angles in-flight. Constant-speed propellers automatically maintain optimal speeds. Feathering propellers minimize drag by aligning blades parallel to airflow, while reverse-pitch propellers facilitate reverse thrust for enhanced control during landing.</p></p>
