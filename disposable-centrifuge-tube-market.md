<p><p>The disposable centrifuge tube market comprises two primary types: round bottom centrifuge tubes and conical centrifuge tubes. Round bottom tubes feature a uniform curvature, optimizing sample recovery by allowing easier sedimentation and minimizing sample loss. In contrast, conical centrifuge tubes have a tapered design that facilitates the collection of sediment at the bottom, making them ideal for pelleting cells and separating liquids. Both types are essential for various laboratory applications, ensuring safety and convenience without cross-contamination risks.</p></p>
