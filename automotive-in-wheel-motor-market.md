<p><p>The Automotive In-wheel Motor market includes two primary types: Outer Rotor Type and Inner Rotor Type. Outer Rotor Type motors have the rotor positioned outside the stator, offering higher torque and compact design, ideal for direct integration into wheels. Inner Rotor Type motors feature the rotor inside the stator, providing higher speed and efficiency, suitable for lightweight electric vehicles. Both types enhance vehicle performance, reduce mechanical losses, and support advancements in electric and autonomous vehicle technologies.</p></p>
