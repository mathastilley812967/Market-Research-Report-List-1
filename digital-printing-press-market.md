<p><p>The digital printing press market primarily consists of two main types: inkjet and laser printing. Inkjet printers use liquid ink, delivered through tiny nozzles, to create high-quality images and are ideal for detailed graphics and photos. In contrast, laser printers utilize powdered toner and a laser beam to produce sharp text and images, offering faster print speeds and cost efficiency for high-volume jobs. Each type caters to different printing needs, influencing their application across various industries.</p></p>
