<p><p>The hand-like robot gripper market comprises three main types: pneumatic, hydraulic, and servo-electric. Pneumatic grippers utilize compressed air for operation, offering lightweight designs and fast actuation, making them ideal for high-speed tasks. Hydraulic grippers use fluid pressure to generate strong gripping forces, suitable for heavy-duty applications. Servo-electric grippers employ electric motors for precise control and adaptability, enabling intricate manipulation. Each type serves distinct industrial needs, balancing speed, strength, and precision in robotic automation.</p></p>
