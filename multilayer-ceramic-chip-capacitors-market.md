<p><p>Multilayer Ceramic Chip Capacitors (MLCCs) are categorized into several types based on their temperature coefficients and dielectric properties. X7R capacitors offer good capacitance stability over a wide temperature range, making them suitable for general applications. X5R capacitors provide moderate capacitance stability but are more compact. C0G (NP0) capacitors exhibit excellent stability and low loss, ideal for precision applications. Y5V capacitors exhibit high capacitance but have lower stability and tolerance. Other types may include variations with specific characteristics tailored for niche applications.</p></p>
