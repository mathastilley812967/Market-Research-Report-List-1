<p><p>The tractor scrapers market is divided into two primary types: wheeled and crawler tractor scrapers. Wheeled type tractor scrapers feature a set of wheels, providing enhanced mobility and speed on solid surfaces, making them suitable for large, open areas. In contrast, crawler type tractor scrapers utilize tracked undercarriages, offering superior traction and stability on uneven or muddy terrains, which makes them ideal for more challenging construction sites. Both types serve essential roles in earthmoving and land grading applications.</p></p>
