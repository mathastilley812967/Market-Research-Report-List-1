<p><p>The Triple Insulated Wire (TIW) market comprises two primary types: Standard Type and Self Bonding Type. In contrast, the Self Bonding Type is designed with adhesive properties that eliminate the need for additional bonding agents during assembly, simplifying manufacturing processes. Both types cater to various sectors, including electronics and renewable energy, ensuring efficient thermal management and reducing the risk of short circuits.</p></p>
