<p><p>The high-strength medical adhesive market is categorized into two main types: soft tissue medical adhesives and hard tissue adhesives. Soft tissue adhesives are designed for bonding flexible tissues, such as skin and mucosal membranes, providing secure wound closure and reducing recovery time. Hard tissue adhesives, on the other hand, are formulated for bonding rigid structures like bone and dental applications, offering enhanced durability and stability in orthopedic and dental procedures. Both types are essential for improving patient outcomes and surgical efficiency.</p></p>
