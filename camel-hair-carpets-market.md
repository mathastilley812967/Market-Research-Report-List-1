<p><strong>The Camel Hair Carpets Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Dromedary Camel Hair</li><li>Bactrian Camel Hair</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The camel hair carpet market is primarily divided into two types based on the source of the fiber: Dromedary camel hair and Bactrian camel hair. Dromedary camel hair, derived from the single-humped camel, is softer and lighter, often used in high-quality carpets that emphasize luxury and comfort. In contrast, Bactrian camel hair, sourced from the two-humped camel, is coarser and more durable, making it suitable for rugs that require ruggedness and resilience. Both types cater to unique customer preferences in texture and durability.</p></p>
